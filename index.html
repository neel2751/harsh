About The Ecosystem

Creating Opportunities.
Building Possibilities.

Since 2010, Samvedna has been strengthening sustainable pathways for livelihood, entrepreneurship, accessibility, and social participation for Divyang individuals.

What began as a grassroots initiative has evolved into a growing ecosystem connecting individuals, employers, entrepreneurs, consumers, institutions, and communities.

We believe true inclusion happens when opportunities are accessible, participation is meaningful, and dignity is sustained.
